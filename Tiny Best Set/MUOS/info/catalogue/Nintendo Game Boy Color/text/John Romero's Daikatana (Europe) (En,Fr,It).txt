In 2455 AD, Kage Mishima used the magical powers of Daikatana, the most powerful sword ever forged, to alter time and establish himself as supreme dictator. The player assumes the role of Hiro Miyamoto, a student in the art of Japanese war and one of the few people on Earth who knows what happened. Now it is Hiro's duty to use the Daikatana's power and travel back in time to correct history.
