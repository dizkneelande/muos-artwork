The 108 Stars of Destiny are back! In this game the player takes on the role of the young hero from Citro Village, and with Marica, Liu and Jale (and other more), the player will explore the world looking for the True Chronicles and stop the evil forces of The Order of the One True Way, lead by the One King! Suikoden Tierkreis is a classical turn-based console Role Playing Game with some interesting Ideas, like mini-quest using specific (or not) character for complete it. The world is completely 3D and the game focuses on story elements. Movement is done with the directional pad or stylus. Like most console RPGs the game has the player interacting with NPCs and fighting random encounters. Items and characters can be exchanged with other players through Wi-Fi.
