Farm crops, raise animals, and meet with unique people as you set out on a free and easy country adventure - with Brammie the duck by your side. Enjoy a more realistic farm experience, including raising animals for food. Run out of provisions and both you and your animals may starve. With more than 25 kinds of animals to raise and a generous selection of fruits and veggies to grow, how you farm is up to you. Work efficiently to save up money and supplies. Your interactions with your neighbors - dining with them, inviting them over - affect the way your farm grows and develops. It'll take a lot more time and effort before you can tie the knot!
