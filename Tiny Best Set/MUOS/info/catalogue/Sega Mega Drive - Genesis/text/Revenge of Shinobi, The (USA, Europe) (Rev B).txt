You are Musashi, master ninja. You are a mystic warrior, who sees and know all things. You are deadly! Now you must destroy the vicious army of Neo Zeed, or Naoko, their beautiful prisoner, dies!
Attack with a ninja sword and shurikens! Somersault over fences while hurling deadly knives! Ward off bullets, death stars and junked cars by leaping, twisting and kicking!
Stalk ninjas, musclemen, Kung Fu gangs and armies of machine gunners through 24 treacherous scenes. The Neo Zeed are masters of disguise. But you are Shinobi, a master in the art of stealth. And you have a secret weapon - ninja magic!
