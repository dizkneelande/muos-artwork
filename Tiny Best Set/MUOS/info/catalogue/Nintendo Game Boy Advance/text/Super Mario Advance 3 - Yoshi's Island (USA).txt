Yoshi's Island: Super Mario Advance 3 makes us discover the origin of brothers Mario and Luigi. While a stork brings both babies to their parents, the wizard Kamek hits him to prevent him from reaching his destination because he predicted that Mario and Luigi would be a problem for his master Bowser in the future. Kamek manages to kidnap Luigi but Mario fails on Yoshis Island. The latter get organised so that Mario finds his brother.
