Legend of the Ghost Lion is a Dragon Quest style turn-based role-playing video game. It is an adaptation of a 1988 Japanese movie, "Piramiddo no Kanata ni Howaito Raion Densetsu". The legend of the White Lion tells that one day, a Lion attacked and ravaged the village. The lion was wounded by the spear of a brave soldier and disappeared in a cave. One day, the parents of a young girl named Maria, decided to go and solve the mystery of the White Lion, leaving her alone. They never returned. While searching for her parents, Maria falls into a river and wakes up in a fantasy world.
